Keith – Team Manager & Lead Coach (Muay Thai)

Hey, I’m Keith! My journey in Martial Arts began at the age of five, and from that first strike, I knew, this wasn’t just training. It was war. I’ve battled through TaeKwonDo, Capoeira, Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu, Muay Thai, and Muay Boran, forging my skills with relentless discipline. As a 2nd Dan Black Belt in TaeKwonDo, I built my foundation in precision and technique before taking my fight to Thailand, where I trained and competed under some of the greatest warriors in the world. I earned recognition from the Kru Muay Thai Association and the World Muay Boran Federation, not by chance, but by sheer force of will.

Martial Arts is dominance. It’s strategy, endurance, and the drive to crush limitations. My mastery also extends to Wai Kru, the sacred ritual that honors the lineage of warriors and sharpens the mind before battle. It’s more than tradition, it’s respect, focus, and the energy that sets the tone for victory

My mission? To build fighters. To create an arena where raw talent is sharpened, weakness is eradicated, and warriors rise. But I don’t just coach, I lead. I want to show the next generation that strength is earned, that fear is destroyed through relentless effort, and that true power starts from within. This isn’t sport. This isn’t just technique. This is the battleground. Step up, or step aside.
