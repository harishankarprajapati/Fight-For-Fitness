About Us

about us

Fight for fitness offers a comprehensive range of martial arts classes, including Muay Thai, Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu (BJJ), Mixed Martial Arts, Boxing, Kickboxing, Wrestling, and more. With the largest class schedule in Asia, it provides a safe, enjoyable, and supportive environment for all. Fight for Fitness is more than a gym—it’s a community where strength, resilience, and transformation happen. We push each other to grow, break limits, and unleash our full potential. Through martial arts and fitness, we build confidence, discipline, and lifelong friendships. Join us and fight for a healthier, stronger, and better you!

Our Mission

At Fight for Fitness, we empower everyone to unleash their greatness through authentic martial arts, guided by World Champions.

We create a safe, fun, and supportive environment where you can experience the many benefits of martial arts:

Improved fitness and overall health

Confidence, mental strength, and discipline

Practical self-defense skills

Lifelong friendships

Founder’s Message

We built Evolve with a dream—to create a home where everyone is family. From all walks of life, we push each other to grow, overcome challenges, and become stronger. Through martial arts, we gain confidence, discipline, and resilience. Some lose weight, some earn belts, and others break bad habits. Our instructors, once struggling, now thrive, inspiring us daily. Evolve is more than a gym; it’s a community where dreams happen and lives transform. We stand together, proving that fitness is more than physical—it’s mental, emotional, and spiritual. Here’s to fighting for fitness and unleashing greatness together!

Our Vision

We welcome everyone—from complete beginners with no prior experience to advanced competitors looking to elevate their skills. Regardless of age, gender, or fitness level, you’ll receive world-class martial arts instruction with the highest standards of excellence. Our programs are designed for maximum learning in the shortest time possible.
