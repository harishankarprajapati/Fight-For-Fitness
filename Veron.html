VERON – CO-Founder and Personal Trainer

Hey, I’m Veron! Strength, resilience, and determination, these are the pillars of the life I lead. From powering through 160KM century rides and conquering half marathons to training in Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu and Muay Thai, I push my limits because I believe in the power of movement and discipline. But true strength isn’t just physical, it’s the confidence, mindset, and perseverance that carry us through life’s challenges.

As a NASM Certified Personal Trainer, I am dedicated to helping others unlock their potential and build a healthier, stronger, and more empowered version of themselves. As women, we are never weak, we are warriors. Strength comes in many forms, and when we cultivate it from within, we rise above obstacles, break barriers, and redefine what we are capable of. It’s not just about fitness, it’s about transformation, inside and out.
